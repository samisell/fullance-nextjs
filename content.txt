# **Fullance Homes and Properties Limited**

## **About Us**

Fullance Homes and Properties Limited is a dynamic real estate development and consulting company founded on an unwavering commitment to integrity, professionalism, and client success. We are dedicated to restoring trust and transparency to the Nigerian property market by providing secure, high-value, and legally verified real estate opportunities.

Our core expertise lies in identifying and developing properties in high-growth corridors, with active projects in key locations across Lagos and Abuja. We serve as a single point of contact for individuals, families, and investors, both in Nigeria and the diaspora, offering an end-to-end service experience—from land acquisition to luxury living.

**Our Guiding Principle:** Building Trust, Delivering Value
